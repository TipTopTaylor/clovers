About Us

Welcome to The 4 Leaf Clover Store! My name is Taylor and these are my clovers! I've had this strange talent to find them since I was super little and it's my favourite summer pastime. I'll spend hours on long walks staring at the grass looking for them. These magical little plants bring me so much joy and I hope they can bring you some joy too! 🍀

All of the clovers are found naturally (by me!) in Ontario, Canada, with respect for nature. Every clover is unique and natural.
